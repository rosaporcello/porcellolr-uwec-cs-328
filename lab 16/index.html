The Mars Rovers

Mars Rover

A Mars rover is a motor vehicle designed to travel on the surface of Mars. Rovers have several advantages over stationary landers: they examine more territory, can be directed to interesting features, and can position themselves optimally for sunlight.

Science

Mars rover science focuses on evidence of past water activity. Rocks, minerals, and geologic landforms hold clues about ancient water processes.
