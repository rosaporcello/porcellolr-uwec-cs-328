Jalapeño Poppers Recipe

Ingredients

  • 12 fresh jalapeño peppers
  • 8 oz cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 6 slices bacon, cooked and crumbled
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Optional: chopped green onions for garnish

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Slice the jalapeños in half lengthwise and remove seeds and membranes carefully.
  3. In a bowl, mix cream cheese, shredded cheddar, crumbled bacon, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
  4. Fill each jalapeño half with the cheese mixture.
  5. Place the stuffed jalapeños on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  6. Bake for 20 minutes or until the jalapeños are tender and the cheese is bubbly and golden.
  7. Let cool slightly, garnish with chopped green onions if desired, and serve warm.
