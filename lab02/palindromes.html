Lab 2: Opposites

Opposites are not contradictory but complementary.

Here are some common pairs of opposites:

  • Big vs. Little
  • High vs. Low
  • Bright vs. Dark
  • Hot vs. Cold
  • Near vs. Far
  • Heavy vs. Light

Reversing ideas can be as simple as using subtle shifts in meaning or as complex as flipping the entire concept upside down.
