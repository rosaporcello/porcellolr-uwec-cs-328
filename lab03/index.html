What is Astrology?

Astrology is the study of celestial bodies and how their positions affect human behavior and events. It is often used for guidance, self-discovery, and understanding others.

The most popular system in the West is the zodiac, which includes 12 signs, each associated with a range of dates and personality traits.

The Four Elements

Each zodiac sign is associated with one of four elements, which influence the sign's characteristics.

  • Fire - Aries, Leo, Sagittarius
  • Earth - Taurus, Virgo, Capricorn
  • Air - Gemini, Libra, Aquarius
  • Water - Cancer, Scorpio, Pisces
  • Each element group shares common personality traits.
