How to Care for Your Crystals

Home Types Care

Crystals absorb energy and should be regularly cleansed to restore their natural properties.

  1. Moonlight: Leave them under a full moon overnight.
  2. Salt Water: (Not for all crystals) Soak them briefly.
  3. Smudging: Use sage or palo santo.
  4. Sound: Use singing bowls or tuning forks.
